Haddley Optometry — WebXR

In VR: right-hand pinch/trigger toggles the exam lights, left-hand pinch/trigger (or grip) cycles the prism simulation — the current strength shows at the bottom of the eye chart. On desktop: drag to look, L lights, P prism, [/] fine-tune.

Laptop + colored-lens (anaglyph) glasses

Put on your 3D glasses, then check the two patches below — cover one eye at a time: each eye should clearly see the patch on its own side and the other patch should look dark. If it's the other way round, press Swap eyes.

Note: ChromaDepth glasses (rainbow/prism lenses) look similar but are not two-color filters and will not work. On a laptop the tests are indicative — screen size and viewing distance aren't calibrated — and a headset (a used Quest 2 works) remains the recommended instrument. Click = trigger; drag = look around.
